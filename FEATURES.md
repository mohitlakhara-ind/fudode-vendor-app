# Product Features - Fudode Vendor App

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the modules and features built into the Fudode Vendor Application.

## 1. Dashboard & Real-Time Orders
The core of the vendor experience is the real-time order management system.

- **Orders List**: View live, preparing, and ready orders with distinct status colors (`New`, `Preparing`, `Ready`).
- **Status Updates**: Simple status transitions for orders to keep customers informed.
- **Order Details**: View customer details, specialized instructions, and a full itemized breakdown.

## 2. Catalog & Menu Management
The catalog system allows vendors to manage their offerings with a multi-level hierarchy.

- **Category Management**: Create and organize top-level categories (e.g., "Main Course").
- **Sub-Category Selection**: Further group items for easier customer discovery (e.g., "Biryani").
- **Item Details**:
  - Image management via `Expo Image Picker`.
  - Item availability (In Stock / Out of Stock).
  - Price and tax configuration.
- **Variants & Add-ons**: Support for item customizations like "Size" or "Extra Cheese".

## 3. Growth & Analytics
Empowering vendors to understand their business performance.

- **Sales Analytics**: View daily, weekly, and monthly revenue trends.
- **Popular Items**: Identify top-selling products.
- **Customer Feedback**: Manage and respond to customer reviews.
- **Campaigns & Ads**: Tools to create promotions and run targeted advertisements to boost visibility.

## 4. Finance & Payouts
Transparent financial tracking for the vendor.

- **Earnings Overview**: View total settled and pending earnings.
- **Payout History**: Detailed logs of historical payouts.
- **Bank Account Management**: Configuration for direct deposit or other settlement methods.

## 5. Outlet & Profile Settings
Administrative tools for managing the physical store and digital presence.

- **Outlet Information**: Update store name, description, and contact details.
- **Timings**: Manage opening/closing hours and holiday configurations.
- **User Management**: Invite other staff members and manage permissions.
- **Legal & Compliance**: Access to terms of service and privacy policies.

## 6. Onboarding & Verification (KYC)
A streamlined process for new vendors to join the platform.

- **Step-by-step Registration**: Guided collection of business details.
- **KYC Status Tracking**: Real-time updates on verification progress.
- **Identity Verification**: Secure document uploads for business validation.
